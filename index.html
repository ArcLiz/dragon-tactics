Who, What and Why?

Welcome, I am so very glad that you've found your way to my corner of the world wide web. My name is Seraphina Goldenstone and I am the Founder and CEO of Dragon Tactics, a limited liability company dedicated to helping individuals across the globe with training their dragons and increasing their own education and knowledge about the magical creatures.

Background

Ever since I was a child, I've been drawn to magical creatures. Dragons, unicorns, phoenixes. I loved them all. There were many times where my mother had to look for me high and low, in the forest, in a pile of books.. I would be searching for the creatures anywhere I could. After a while, I admitted to myself that it would be very difficult to get a hold of any of these creatures, so instead I surrounded myself with furry pets. I worked my way from hamsters to cats and lastly up to dogs, and when I got my first dog I realized my love of training animals to harness both their mental and physical capabilities.

Life Calling

When raising and training dogs became as easy as breathing, I knew that I needed a change. So I started educating myself in the art of dragon training. I began learning from movies and books, but after a while I did some big logic brain magicz and made logicz assumptions.

My logic went like this: A dragon flies and is the same size as a small plane, so he/she/it/they should need to adhere to regular air and flight protocols. The same goes with their fire or frost breath, weather controlling capabilities etc. So, I just studied all those things via wikipedia and voil√°, all-knowing in the art of dragon training, maintenance and regulation I became!
